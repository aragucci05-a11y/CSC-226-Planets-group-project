Welcome to Planet Seeker!
Here you can view the phases of each planet of the solar system as viewed from Earth!


A phase angle is the angle between the Sun, the observed object, and the observer (e.g., Earth).
It determines how much of the object's surface is illuminated and visible from the observer's perspective.
